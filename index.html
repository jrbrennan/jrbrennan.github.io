Work History and Bio

For most of my career, I have worked in the transportation industry for a data collection firm. Through that work, I have gained experience in Operations Management and Information Technology and have held positions at the manager, director, and executive levels. Most recently, I have been helping build software for transportation data management systems as a product manager and programmer writing automated API tests using Python.

In the early part of my career, I worked in academia as an economics professor and researcher, including teaching microeconomic theory, game theory, and econometrics. More recently, I had an opportunity to combine some of my knowledge of economics and finance with my experience on software development projects as I did product consulting work for a fintech startup building a direct-to-investor wealth management platform. In this role, I built spreadsheet-based financial tools for investing, portfolio risk quantification, risk tolerance assessment, and cashflow planning.
